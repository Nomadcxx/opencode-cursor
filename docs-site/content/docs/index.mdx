opencode-cursor connects OpenCode to the models available through your Cursor
subscription. It translates prompts, streaming responses, and tool calls
between OpenCode and `cursor-agent`.

## Start here

- [Install opencode-cursor](/docs/getting-started/installation) and check that
  OpenCode can see the provider.
- [Run your first prompt](/docs/getting-started/quick-start) with
  `cursor-acp/auto`.
- [Work through a failure](/docs/getting-started/troubleshooting) by symptom.
